About Happy Child

HAPPY Green Apple Gloabal and Rehabilitation Centre is a comprehensive outpatient rehabilitation centre located in Vijayawada. We are dedicated to helping children and adolescents along with their families to maximize the ability of the child to participate in activities at home, school & the community and empower parents with the knowledge & skill required to further enhance their child's development
